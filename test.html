Game Equipment

The Engine powers the entire game world. You can use your Sword to attack enemies and your Shield to defend against their attacks. When your health is low, drink a Potion to recover.

Game Tutorial

Welcome to the game! First, let's learn about the basic equipment:

  • Your Engine determines how fast you can move
  • The Sword is your primary weapon
  • Use your Shield wisely to block incoming attacks
  • Keep some Potions handy for emergencies

Game Tips

Here are some tips for beginners:

  • Upgrade your Engine regularly to move faster
  • Practice using your Shield to time blocks perfectly
  • Don't waste your Potions - save them for boss fights
  • Keep your Sword sharp for maximum damage
